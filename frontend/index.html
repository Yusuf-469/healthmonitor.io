How It Works

Simple 3-Step Process to Get Started

Start monitoring patients in just a few minutes

01

Connect Sensors

Attach health sensors like heart rate (MAX30102), temperature (DS18B20), and blood pressure sensors to your ESP32 microcontroller.

  • MAX30102 Heart Rate & SpO2
  • DS18B20 Temperature
  • Blood Pressure Sensor
02

Connect to Cloud

Device transmits data securely to our cloud platform via WiFi in real-time. All data is encrypted and stored safely.

  • Real-time Transmission
  • End-to-End Encryption
  • Automatic Backups
03

Monitor & Alert

View live dashboards and receive instant alerts when health metrics are abnormal. AI predicts issues before they occur.

  • Live Dashboard
  • Instant Notifications
  • AI Predictions
